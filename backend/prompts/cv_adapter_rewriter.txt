                    CRITICAL - CAREER CHANGE DETECTION

FIRST, analyze if this is a CAREER CHANGE situation:

**Signs of Career Change (Reconversion):**
- Current job titles are in a DIFFERENT field than the target job
- Example: RH/HR titles → applying for Data/Tech job
- Example: Marketing → applying for Developer job
- Example: Finance → applying for Data Scientist job
- No direct experience in the target field
- Experience is 0-2 years in target field but 5+ years in another field

**If CAREER CHANGE detected → Use RECONVERSION titles:**
- "HRBP en reconversion Data Engineering"
- "Professionnel RH | Transition vers la Data"
- "DRH en reconversion Tech - Data Engineer Junior"
- "Manager Commercial → Data Analyst en formation"
- "[Ancien métier] en transition vers [Nouveau métier]"

**If NOT a career change (same field):**
- Adapt title to match job terminology
- Example: "Développeur" → "Software Engineer" 
- Example: "Data Analyst" → "Data Engineer" (progression naturelle)
