**Header (top-left):**

**Header (top-right):**

- City, Date (format: "Paris, le 5 février 2025")

**Subject Line:**

"Madame, Monsieur,"

**Paragraph 1 - Current Situation (STRICT: 2-3 sentences max):**
- Who you are + current status in ONE sentence

**Paragraph 2 - Skills & Experience (STRICT: 3 sentences max):**
- ONE sentence: your strongest relevant skill + one concrete project/achievement with number if possible
- ONE sentence: technical match with the job requirements (mention 2-3 specific technologies)
- ONE sentence: what you bring concretely to their team

**Paragraph 3 - Motivation for Role & Company (STRICT: 2 sentences max):**
- ONE sentence: why this specific company/mission excites you (be specific, no generic praise)
- ONE sentence: your enthusiasm + forward-looking closing

**Closing:**
"Dans l'attente de votre retour, je vous prie d'agréer, Madame, Monsieur, l'expression de mes salutations distinguées."

**Signature:**
[First Name Last Name]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    STYLE RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. **ONE PAGE ONLY** - Total body text: 7-8 sentences maximum across all 3 paragraphs. This is a hard limit.
2. **Be Specific** - Mention specific projects, technologies, company products
3. **Be Concise** - Max 280 words for the 3 paragraphs combined (excluding header/closing)
4. **Be Professional** - Formal but not stiff
5. **No Clichés** - Avoid "passionné", "motivé", "dynamique" alone
6. **Show Don't Tell** - Instead of "je suis motivé", explain WHY you're excited
7. **Match Language** - Use same language as job posting
8. **Include Project Names** - Reference actual projects from CV with links if available
9. **NO PADDING** - Every sentence must add value. If a sentence is generic, delete it.
